The Gap We Exist to Close

Too Large to Operate Without HR.
Too Small to Build It Internally.

India has 3.1 million businesses with 50–1,000 employees. Nearly every one has an HR problem — not from lack of care, but from a structural gap: too large to operate without HR, too small to build it internally.

They need strategic HR leadership but are paying for administrative support. They need cultural architecture but are getting policy documentation.

The Mélange Answer

One partner. The full people function. Zero distinction between "Mélange team" and "our HR team."

We operate as the embedded HR leadership layer — attending SLT meetings, running compliance calendars, managing HR issues in real time.

The Mélange HR Method

Three Layers.
One People Operating System.

Most HR vendors deliver outputs. The Mélange HR Method builds an integrated people operating system — three layers, one relationship.

Layer One
Compliance
The floor that protects everything above it

PF, ESI, TDS, POSH, labour law — built properly, first. No one builds high-performance culture on a compliance foundation that is cracked.

Layer Two
Operations
The engine that makes HR run smoothly

Payroll, HRSS, recruitment, exit management, HRMS, analytics — the operational layer that gives visibility into people data and the ability to act.

Layer Three
Culture
The environment that determines whether people stay

HRBP-as-a-Service, ESAT, performance management, employer branding — closing the gap between the culture leaders believe they have and the one employees actually experience.

Eight Service Lines

One Integrated
People Operating System

Eight moments in the HR lifecycle — delivered as a single integrated relationship, not a collection of disconnected vendors. Every service line connects. Every data point informs the whole.

01
HRSS
HR Shared Services

The administrative backbone — SLA-governed, structured, and run with the judgement of a senior professional.

  • HR helpdesk — queries answered within 24 hours
  • Policy drafting, review, and version control
  • Offer, confirmation, warning, exit documentation
  • Digital employee records management
  • Monthly HR MIS: headcount, attrition, compliance
  • Quarterly HR Health Check
Best for: Startups at 30–200 employees
02
Payroll
Payroll Processing & Statutory Compliance

Accurate. On time. Compliant. Every month. One missed filing triggers penalties that compound daily — we prevent that.

  • Full-cycle payroll: gross-to-net, deductions, arrears, LOP
  • Digital payslip generation — every employee, every month
  • TDS computation, quarterly Form 24Q, annual Form 16
  • PF, ESI, PT, LWF — monthly remittance and filing
  • Full & Final settlement within 30 days of exit
  • Monthly payroll audit and reconciliation
Target: 100% payroll accuracy — non-negotiable
03
POSH & Legal
Statutory, Legal & POSH Compliance

POSH non-compliance is personal liability for directors. Most organisations under 300 employees have never constituted an ICC.

  • ICC constitution — formally appointing required members
  • POSH policy drafting — specific, legally compliant
  • Annual POSH training — live, facilitated, with records
  • Complaint management protocol — documented and rehearsed
  • Annual POSH report filed with the district officer
  • Full statutory compliance calendar management
Non-negotiable: included in all tiers
04
Recruitment
Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

We fill roles with people who can do the job and fit the culture — and will still be there in 18 months.

  • Retained search, contingency, and RPO models
  • Every candidate assessed for professional readiness
  • Linked to Mélange alumni pipeline from Pillar 1
  • Replacement guarantee (30–90 days depending on model)
  • Employer brand advisory included
  • Time-to-fill benchmark: 28 days for mid-level roles
Offer acceptance rate tracked every mandate
05
HRBP
HRBP as a Service

A senior people professional embedded in the business — attending SLT meetings, advising on org design, coaching managers.

  • Attendance at SLT and leadership meetings
  • Organisational design advisory
  • Manager coaching — on actual challenges they face
  • Workforce planning and succession mapping
  • People data interpretation — ESAT, attrition, payroll
  • Change management: restructures, leadership transitions
Metrics: eNPS trajectory + 90-day attrition rate
06
Exit
Exit Management

How people leave shapes who decides to join. Every exit is a brand moment — managed well or managed poorly.

  • Structured exit interview facilitation — surfacing real reasons
  • F&F settlement — computed, documented, within 30 days
  • Clearance management — IT, finance, admin owned by Mélange
  • Attrition analytics — quarterly pattern identification
  • Alumni network seeding into Pillar 3 programme
Metric: Glassdoor rating trend + rehire pipeline
07
ESAT
ESAT, eNPS & Culture Mapping

Mélange ESAT is designed for truth — not manageable data. Administered consistently, translated into actions leadership can execute.

  • Annual ESAT — custom, quantitative and qualitative
  • eNPS — directional trajectory, not just position
  • Quarterly pulse check — 5–8 questions, early warning
  • Culture mapping diagnostic — actual vs. believed
  • People Health Report — quarterly narrative to leadership
  • Action planning — Mélange co-designs the response
Benchmark: eNPS above 25 · Every cycle + debrief
08
PMS
Performance Management Systems

A PMS people actually use is a conversation architecture — not the annual appraisal dreaded in equal measure by everyone in the room.

  • PMS framework design — goals, competencies, rating scales
  • Goal-setting facilitation — Mélange runs the cycle
  • Mid-year review coordination
  • Appraisal cycle management — calibration and moderation
  • Manager coaching on performance conversations
  • PMS analytics — distribution reports, bell curve, equity
Metric: PMS completion rate + manager effectiveness
